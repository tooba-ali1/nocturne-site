Frequently asked questions

Do I need my own telescope?

No. All tours include professional-grade telescope access, shared in small groups so everyone gets real time at the eyepiece.

What if it's cloudy?

Tours are scheduled around seasonal forecasts and new-moon windows. If conditions turn, we reschedule your viewing session or offer a partial refund.

Is this suitable for beginners?

Yes. Most guests have never used a telescope before. Guides start with naked-eye navigation before moving to equipment.

What should I bring?

Warm layers regardless of season — desert and mountain sites drop sharply after dark. We provide red-light headlamps and hot drinks.

How do I book?

Choose a departure above and reach out through the contact section, or ask our chat assistant for real-time availability.
